The joy of cooking (an app)

By Huon Wilson — 30 Aug 2021

Taking shortcuts? Leaving edge-cases unconsidered and unhandled? Is this engineering?! My approach to programming and software engineering has been shaped by years of building open source compilers and libraries, where those edge cases matter, reliability is crucial and flexibility is important. It‚Äôs been a breath of fresh air to take a step back and stop thinking about every detail, and instead write code that works for a specific set of people: me and my wife.

The project

I got married last October, and I wanted to have an anticipatory countdown, somehow. We both happen to have Garmin watches, which support custom watch faces0. I created a simple but fun little app: it shows the time, date and the battery, but also has a photo and a message.

The photo and message changes each day, usually randomly. However, on some special days, it chooses a particular message or photo, like the countdown across the 10 days leading up to our wedding.
